Find the answers to frequently asked questions.

How long does it take to setup my account?
Our virtual private servers are instantly deployed using our billing system. If instant setup fail for any reason, we will setup within 24 hours.
Which Plan Should I Choose?
You can upgrade or downgrade your VPS plan anytime without downtime. If you are not sure which plan will be most suitable for you, we recommend starting small and scaling your way up.
Can I upgrade my plan after I sign up?
Yes, you can upgrade your plan any time without any downtime. Upgrading your plan is easy and free, scale up at any time from your dashboard with a click of a button.
Does ISPbills charge any hidden fees?
We do not charge any hidden fees. All our fees can are clearly published on our website or Terms of Service. However, if you wish to avail of our backup service or any other service that is not included in the vps packages, then the extra charges will apply. Also, the control panel fee will be applied.
Will you transfer over my website?
Transfering from another host? Our expert support team is standing by to transfer your site from your old host. You can read our Transfer Policy here
Does ISPbills perform backups of my account?
Yes, we do offer automated daily backup using Jet Backup, with 14 daily restore points. And you're in full control of when your daily backups take place. Restore a clean version of your website or recover a single file or folder with just one click. However, there is a small fee for the backup service.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept a wide variety of payment methods including Rocket, bKash, Nagad, Bank Transfer, Local and International Credit Card, Paypal etc.
Why don't you offer unlimited storage?
Unlimited Storage is a myth. As everything has limit. We're upfront with our shared hosting plan limits and don't hide hidden limits in our Terms of Service like most unlimited storage providers..
