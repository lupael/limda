Advantages of using our BDIX Web Hosting Services

Saves International Bandwidth

We have multiple IX connectivity like NIX, aamra IX, Novo NIX, Level-3 IX, Kloud IX and especially BDIX which will save your server international bandwidth. The IX only uses local bandwidth which means when a user from an ISP sends a request to another user in another ISP - the data does not require to travel out of the country.

Low Latency

If you use our BDIX connected along with all other IX connected hosting, your site will have extremely low latency. In this case, the local traffic will only travel inside the country, because of all the IX and the BDIX connectivity. There is no need for any international routes to help at all. The latency will be mostly between 1 to 10 milliseconds.

High Data Volume

BDIX Connectivity can assist you in transferring large amounts of data. You will obtain the best transfer rate because of Inter Exchange. Our BDIX Hosting now offers up to 10 Gbps connectivity. This will help you with the fastest High Data Volume Transfer rate possible.

Topnotch Performance
