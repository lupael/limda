How long does it take to setup my account?
Our web hosting are instantly deployed using our billing system. If instant setup fail for any reason, we will setup within 24 hours.
Which Plan Should I Choose?
You can upgrade or downgrade your hosting plan anytime without downtime. If you are not sure which plan will be most suitable for you, we recommend starting small and scaling your way up.
Can I upgrade my plan after I sign up?
Yes, you can upgrade your plan at any time without any downtime. Upgrading your plan is easy and free, scale up at any time from your dashboard with just a click.
Does ISPbills charge any hidden fees?
No, we do not charge any hidden fees. You only pay for the plans and add-ons you select at signup, whether you decide to take advantage of one of our Black Friday specials or get our web hosting at the regular price. Every month, you will also receive an invoice that details all of the services you are being charged for.
Would You Provide Customer Support?
Yes, we have. One of the main advantages of selecting ISPbills as your web hosting provider is that you have 24/7 access to our highly skilled crew. Send us a message at any time, no matter what the issue is; we are always available and pleased to assist you.
Is it possible to migrate My Website to ISPbills?
Yes, our expert support team is standing by to transfer your site from your old host. Contact us and describe your website in detail, along with the content management system you use and the plan you are considering. You could save money on your hosting plan when you move during the Black Friday web hosting sales.
Does ISPbills perform backups of my account?
Yes, we do offer automated daily backup on shared hosting using Jet Backup, with 14 daily restore points. And you're in full control of when your daily backups take place. Restore a clean version of your website – or recover a single file or folder – with just one click.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept a wide variety of payment methods including Rocket, bKash, Nagad, Bank Transfer, Local and International Credit Card, Paypal etc.
Why don't you offer unlimited storage?
