Payment method

Simply make your payment via internet or mobile banking, and ISPbills's billing system will update your bill payment once you have created a support ticket using the transaction ID. Here is a step-by-step guide to paying online.

Mobile banking payment

Make your payment using mobile banking directly. (Please note Mobile banking payment applicable for Bangladesh only).

  1. Go to your bKash account Menu by dialing *247# USSD

    Payment by dialing bKash USSD code

    Send your payment to our bKash merchant account given below. Copy the number & follow the next steps.

  2. Select "Payment" option from the bKash menu

    You'll need to select the payment option from the menu

    Enter our bKash wallet number that you copied "01711-928149".

  3. Enter the amount you have to pay

    Amount to pay in order to activate your service

    Check your invoice and enter the amount you need to pay to ISPbills.

  4. Enter a reference against your transaction & counter number in the next step

    Enter a reference number and then enter a counter number

    Reference number will be your payment invoice number. Counter number will be "1".

  5. Now enter your PIN to confirm

    Enter your secured PIN code

    To complete your payment, enter your 4 or 5 digit secured PIN code
